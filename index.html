Rock climbing is an adrenaline-fueling sport that involves ascending steep rock formations using specialized equipment and techniques. It requires strength, flexibility, endurance, and mental focus as climbers navigate challenging routes, often hundreds of feet off the ground.

Rock climbing has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century, and has evolved into a popular pastime for adventurers, fitness enthusiasts, and competitive athletes.
